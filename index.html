About Me

Hello! My name is Arun CK. My journey began with a foundation in Physics, Mathematics, and Electronics, which sparked a passion for logic and problem-solving. I went on to earn my Master's in Graphics and Animation, where I learned to merge my technical skills with creative expression. This unique combination allows me to create projects that are not only visually compelling but also technically robust. I specialize in graphic design, type design, and animation, with a strong focus on using technology to celebrate and preserve cultural heritage. I am driven by the belief that design can be a powerful tool for storytelling and community building.
